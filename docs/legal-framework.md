# Legal Framework Pack (Platform-First)

This file outlines the legal protection posture for Supply & Demand and maps it to product controls.

## Goals
- Maximize platform protection against operational and legal risk.
- Preserve balanced baseline protections for consumers and suppliers.
- Ensure enforceability through explicit acceptance flows.

## High-Protection Clauses Included
- Disclaimer of warranties and no-guarantee language.
- Limitation of liability with low monetary cap.
- Broad user/supplier indemnification obligations.
- Binding arbitration and class/collective action waiver.
- Broad suspension and termination rights for risk exposure.
- Setoff and clawback rights from supplier payouts.
- Escrow hold discretion for fraud/compliance concerns.
- Verification and re-verification rights with document audits.
- Strict prohibited conduct and anti-circumvention rules.

## Product Integration Requirements
- Add mandatory checkboxes during signup and supplier onboarding.
- Store `terms_version`, `accepted_at`, and `ip_address` in audit logs.
- Block supplier activation until legal acceptance is complete.
- Trigger forced re-acceptance on policy version changes.
- Add legal links in footer, trust pages, onboarding flows, and payment confirmation screens.

## Recommended Next Legal Hardening
- Add jurisdiction-specific annexes (US, UK, EU, Caribbean regions).
- Add DPA and subprocessors list for enterprise partners.
- Add sanctions and export control policy.
- Add DMCA/IP takedown policy and repeat-infringer workflow.
- Add tax language by market (VAT/GST/withholding).
- Add marketplace facilitator clauses where applicable.

## Important
Templates are operationally strong but must be reviewed by licensed counsel for jurisdiction-specific enforceability.
