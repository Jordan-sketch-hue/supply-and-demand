Supplier Agreement

Effective date: March 6, 2026

1. Independent Business Relationship

Supplier is an independent contractor and not an employee, partner, franchisee, or agent of the platform. No authority to bind the platform is granted.

2. Mandatory Compliance

Supplier must maintain valid licenses, permits, certifications, and insurance required by law and category standards. Supplier bears sole responsibility for legal compliance.

3. Verification and Audit Rights

The platform may request documents at onboarding and periodically. Failure to provide requested evidence may result in immediate suspension, payout holds, or termination.

4. Service Standards

Supplier must provide accurate listings, truthful pricing, professional conduct, timely communication, and lawful fulfillment. Misrepresentation is a material breach.

5. Commission, Fees, and Setoff

Supplier authorizes deduction of commissions, taxes where required, promotion charges, refunds, chargebacks, fines, and investigation costs from current or future payouts.

6. Insurance and Liability Coverage

Supplier must keep commercially reasonable insurance for their activity class and name required parties as additional insured where mandated in final legal package.

7. Data and Confidentiality

Supplier may use customer data only for transaction fulfillment and must not sell, reuse, or disclose personal information beyond lawful operational necessity.

8. Indemnity and Risk Allocation

Supplier indemnifies platform entities from claims related to services, products, injuries, property damage, labor issues, taxes, compliance violations, and third-party disputes.

9. Termination and Survival

Termination may occur at any time for policy, trust, legal, or reputational risk. Indemnity, payment recovery, confidentiality, and dispute provisions survive termination.
