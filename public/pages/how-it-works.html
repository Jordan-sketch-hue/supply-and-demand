How It Works

1. Search Your Demand

Users submit natural language requests like "I need a mechanic in Montego Bay now". The AI parser extracts category, location, urgency, and intent.

2. Match Trusted Supply

The matching engine ranks suppliers using proximity, trust score, reviews, response speed, and service fit.

3. Book & Protect the Transaction

Payment enters escrow, booking confirms availability, and completion is verified before payout release.
