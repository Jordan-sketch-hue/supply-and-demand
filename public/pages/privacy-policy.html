Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

Account data, identity and verification data, transaction records, communications, location signals, device identifiers, behavioral analytics, payment metadata, and fraud indicators.

2. Why We Use Data

To operate matching services, process bookings and payments, enforce policies, detect fraud, resolve disputes, meet legal obligations, and protect platform rights and safety.

3. Legal Bases

Contract performance, legitimate interests, legal compliance, and consent where required by applicable law.

4. Data Sharing

We share data with payment providers, verification partners, anti-fraud vendors, analytics providers, customer support tools, legal authorities, and transaction counterparties as needed.

5. Cross-Border Transfers

Data may be transferred internationally using contractual safeguards and security controls suitable for cross-border processing.

6. Data Retention

Retention depends on legal, tax, accounting, anti-fraud, and dispute requirements. Data may be retained after account closure where lawful and necessary.

7. User Rights

Depending on jurisdiction, users may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or objection. Some requests may be denied where legal duties or security interests apply.

8. Automated Decisioning

Fraud, trust, and ranking systems may use algorithmic signals. We reserve rights to restrict accounts or transactions based on risk models, with manual review pathways where required.

9. Security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards, but no method is fully secure. Users must protect credentials and report suspected compromise promptly.
